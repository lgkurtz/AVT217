It's not the best Crab. It's Chesapeake Crab

Our menu showcases the bounty of the Chesapeake Bay watershed in thoughtful, delicious and beautiful dishes that aim to nourish and surprise. The food is accompanied by a program of fine craft cocktails and an expertly curated hard-seltzer list. Each week we aim to surprise our patrons with a truly unique dining experience. Whether it’s live music, or a community viewing of a movie or TV show, we’re make sure your night out will be made memorable. At Pines of Nowhere we’re committed to giving our newly adult patrons the most fun possible restaurant going experience!
